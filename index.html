Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Zhejiang University & Westlake University joint Ph.D. program, located in Hangzhou, China. I am fortunately supervised by Prof. Yue Zhang, WestlakeNLP Lab.

My research primarily focuses on Large Language Models and Retrieval, especially Retrieval-Augmented LLMs Generation and LLM-as-Retriever.

Till now, I have authored 16 papers, including 12 first/co-first author papers, with 4 published in ACL, 1 in Neurips and 1 in EMNLP, 1 in Semeval Task Track, 3 in NLPCC and 2 in submission. I have also got the Best Task Paper Award Honorable Mention at Semeval2020 as the first author.

I am on track to receive my PhD degree in June 2024. Currently, I'm actively seeking opportunities as a research scientist or postdoc. Please don't hesitate to reach out!
